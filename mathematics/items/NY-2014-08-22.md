To calculate annual compound interest, Sheba can use the
formula

$$A = P{(1 + r)}^{y}$$

$$A = 36500{(1 + 0.07)}^{20}$$

$$A = 36500{(1.07)}^{20}$$

$$A = \$ 141,243$$
